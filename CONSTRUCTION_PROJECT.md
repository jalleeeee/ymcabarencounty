## 1. Project Scope

The project encompasses the design, permitting, and full construction of a new YMCA community recreation center in Baren County. The facility will provide fitness, aquatics, youth programming, and community event space to serve county residents of all ages.

## 3. Project Phases & Schedule

- [ ] Site survey and geotechnical investigation

- [ ] Structural, MEP, and civil engineering plans
- [ ] Environmental impact assessment
- [ ] Permit applications (building, grading, stormwater, etc.)
- [ ] Contractor bidding and selection

### Phase 2 — Site Preparation (Months 5–6)
- [ ] Site clearing and grubbing
- [ ] Demolition of existing structures (if applicable)
- [ ] Rough grading and excavation
- [ ] Underground utility installation (water, sewer, electric, gas)
- [ ] Erosion and sediment control measures in place

### Phase 3 — Foundation & Structure (Months 7–10)
- [ ] Foundation footings and slab pour
- [ ] Structural steel or framing erection
- [ ] Roof decking and waterproofing

### Phase 4 — Enclosure & MEP Rough-In (Months 11–14)
- [ ] Exterior cladding, windows, and doors installation
- [ ] Roofing system installation
- [ ] HVAC ductwork and equipment rough-in
- [ ] Electrical conduit and panel rough-in
- [ ] Plumbing rough-in (pool, locker rooms, restrooms)
- [ ] Fire suppression system rough-in
- [ ] Insulation installation

### Phase 5 — Interior Finishes (Months 15–18)

- [ ] Interior painting

## 6. Safety Requirements

- All contractors must comply with OSHA 29 CFR 1926 Construction Safety Standards.
- Site-specific Safety Plan required from the General Contractor prior to mobilization.
- Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) mandatory on site at all times.
- Daily safety briefings required during active construction phases.
- Incident and near-miss reporting procedures established before work begins.
- Site access restricted to authorized personnel with posted signage.
- Regular safety inspections conducted by the Site Safety Officer.

---

## 7. Quality Control

- Inspections at each phase milestone per the project schedule.
- Third-party special inspections required for: concrete, structural steel, soils/compaction, and waterproofing.
- All materials and equipment submittals reviewed and approved by the Architect and Engineer of Record.
- RFI (Request for Information) and submittal logs maintained by the Project Manager.
